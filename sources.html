Why does this exist?

This first version of this webpage became a reality when I decided to have some fun and maybe showcase a few of my personal endevours.
As I began looking for a job in my freshmen year of college, however, I became frustrated with how little a resume and a few checkboxes
could tell my story. My fun project eventually morphed this webpage where I could show of some projects I had fun with and go into more
detail about who I am and what people could expect from me.
